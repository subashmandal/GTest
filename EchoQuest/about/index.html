EchoQuest - The Voice Recognition Game

Challenge your pronunciation skills and have fun while learning new words.

About EchoQuest

EchoQuest is an exciting educational game that uses voice recognition to help you learn and improve your pronunciation of different words. Whether you're a language learner or just looking for a fun challenge, EchoQuest has something for everyone.

Game Modes

  • Free Mode: Play without limits and practice as much as you like.
  • Challenge Mode: Test your skills in a timed challenge where every mistake could cost you the game.
  • Difficulty Levels: Choose from Easy, Normal, or Hard to match your skill level.

How to Play

Simply start the game and follow the on-screen instructions. Speak the words clearly to score points. The more accurate your pronunciation, the higher your score!
